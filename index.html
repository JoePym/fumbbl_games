fumbbl_games by Hitonagashi

Webpages of the Blackbox dataset.
This dataset gives 2 numbers. The percentage is the percentage of games won by that race at that TV bracket against that TV. Read along the rows, so each row gives the win percentage for each race. The smaller figure is the number of games played in that range by that combination. 960 TV or less games
960-1210 TV games
1210-1460 TV games
1460-1710 TV games
1710-1960 TV games
1960-2210 TV games
2210-2460 TV games
2460-2710 TV games
2710 TV and above games
